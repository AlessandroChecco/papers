Classify Sizing/Fitting Issue In Fashion Item Reviews (Eng) - Less Than 2 Minutes Per Task (V.0.99)

Instructions

Overview

In this job, you will be presented with reviews related to fashion items (only to shoes). You need to highlight specific parts related to  "Size or Fit" aspect of the review.

We are interested in capturing the language people use to express sentiment about "Size or Fit". We will ask you to highlight some part of the text where people express their opinion. We are also interested in the opinion sentiment: positive, negative or neutral (sentiment only regarding size or fit, not general or other topics). For example "size is too small" (negative sentiment), "fitted very well" (positive sentiment) etc.

Steps

  1. Read the review. Determine if the comment discuss about "Size or Fit", for example:
    • The correct size has been delivered, but the item is bigger or smaller than expected (e.g, “size 6 is too small for me”) 
    • The item doesn't fit (e.g, “the sleeves are too long”). This can happen even if the item received has the correct size.
  2. Label the Sentiment expression of the opinion. You should label the portion of text with the correct color depending on the sentiment (positive, neutral or negative). For example in the sentence "size 6 is too small for me" you should label with red marker next words: "size 6 is too small for me" (negative sentiment). 

Rules & Tips:

  • When you highlight, select whole words. Don't worry about the white spaces or punctuation on the boundary.
  • We are interested in "Size or Fit" aspect related to an item itself, not about shopping or delivery experience. Most valuable reviews are the one where customers express Sentiment or describe a Problem regarding "Size or Fit" aspect.
  • Comfort of wearing an item is different aspect than "Size or Fit". Imagine situation, when you try on some shoes in the shop and you express you opinion about size and fit, thi is a situation we are interested in.
  • If you can not understand the sentiment (positive, neutral, negative) of the review with regard to "Size or Fit" aspect, mark it with "Other" class. This could be in a situation when customer writes some general remark, personal context, delivery, price or other aspects.
  • If the review mention several "Size or Fit" aspects, label all of them.
  • Matching size considered as a positive sentiment, and unmatching as negative. Just expressing the fact "the shoe is wide" and when it is not clear from the context if it is good or bad, then it should be considered neutral.
  • Sentiment expressions should be highlighted including these words:
    • Intencity words: "really", "very" and similar
    • Negations words: "not", "disappointed" and similar
    • Certainty words: "very", "really", "hardly"and similar
    • Neutralizers: "could", "should", "ought to"and similar
  • References to specific people should not be highlighted: "this shoe fitted well for my son." (do not highlight "for my son").
  • Conclusion or consequences should not be highlighted: "size was too small, that is why I can not were it"
  • When the item displayed is not a shoe or the language is not English, please use "Data error" checkbox.

Examples

Classes

Highlighting Examples

Size aspect

In these examples, the comment is expressing a  feedback about the item's size. When the sentiment is negative, the item's size is either too large or too small compared to the regular one. Description of size can be labels M, L, XL and numbers for apparel; numbers for shoes (43, 44,..); children size usually talk about age.
  • "Fantastic! Shipping was fast. Can't wait to shop here again! These shoes are around one size larger than normal."
  • "Large fit"
  • "bigger than expected"
  • "this is not XL"
  • "way too HUGE"
  • "Wrong sizes"
  • "lovely top but way too big"
  • "the size is OK"
  • "I recommend to buy the this shoes one size biger"

Fit aspect

In these examples, the comment is expressing a  feedback about the item's fit, but it does not specify a size issue. The problem could be related to a comfortability regarding fitting aspect.
  • "doesn't fit"
  • "The shoes really rub on your ankle"
  • "Perfect fit"

Other

In these examples, the comments are not related to the sizing or fitting aspect. Note that delivering the wrong size or missing a size in the inventory is not a size aspect.
  • "boots arrived 6 days after ordering which was fine - but they had sent me the wrong style and the wrong size !!!" - (Related to delivery)
  • "great shoes" - (General remark)
  • "Nice Perfume"  - (Data error )
  • "the sleeves are too long" - (Data error)
  • "Very comfortable shoes" - (Comfortability remark)
  • "loved the jacket but a shame no larger sizes were available" - (Data error)
  • "There is no my size available on the web site" - (Shopping issue)
